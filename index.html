Who I Am

I'm Sophie, a dynamic problem-solver based in sunny San Diego, with a background in teaching high school math and a passion for empowering others to persist through challenges by helping to build a toolkit of diverse problem-solving methods. As a developer, I've built proficiency in Javascript, Python SQL, crafted responsive user interfaces with React and Redux, and built robust backends with Flask and Express. Building three projects from scratch, including a comprehensive travel app, reflects my passion for innovation and taking abstract visions and generating tangible solutions. I bring a unique blend of skills honed in teaching, such as attention to detail, time management, leadership, and constructive feedback. My journey signifies not just a career shift but a continuation of my identity as a solution-oriented professional, critical thinker, and lifelong learner. I am ready and excited to contribute meaningfully to the world of software development.
