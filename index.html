Who I Am

Hi, nice to "meet" you! I'm a dynamic problem-solver based in the sunny San Diego. My professional journey has always been driven by finding immediate needs within communities and developing solutions that make a direct impact. I originally pursued a career in teaching math because I believe that education is one of the most powerful tools for empowerment and social change and there is a critical need for quality math education. Teaching math allowed me to build critical thinking and problem-solving skills in students, equipping them with tools they could use to navigate the world around them. I transitioned to software engineering after realizing the potential to scale my impact beyond the classroom. My experience with educational software highlighted the transformative power of technology, inspiring me to contribute to innovative solutions that address broader societal needs. As a developer, I've built three projects from inception to production which reflects my passion for innovation and taking abstract visions and generating tangible outcomes. My journey signifies not just a career shift but a continuation of my identity as a solution-oriented professional, critical thinker, and lifelong learner. I'm ready and excited to contribute meaningfully to the world of software development!

Projects I've Built

SplitTrip

SplitTrip, built with a Flask-React stack, is a travel platform blending TripAdvisor's exploration and planning features with Splitwise's expense management capabilities. Offering a one-stop solution, users can discover, plan, and book their trips while efficiently managing travel expenses in a stress and hassle-free environment.

Parmazon Prime, built with a PostgreSQL Flask backend and a React/Redux frontend, is a delectable twist on the shopping experience, bringing the charm of Amazon to the world of artisanal cheeses. This platform allows users to easily explore and order an array of gourmet options with the same ease and reliability as their favorite online marketplace.

Meet U

My first project! Meet U, drawing inspiration from MeetUp and built with Express.js and React/Redux, is a event-based social networking platform designed to connect like-minded individuals for a variety of events and activities. Users are able to create and join groups in order to create meaningful connections and create and attend events in order to foster shared experiences that make every encounter a delightful "Nice to MEET U".
